Target Practice


This project was a rather personal one. It consisted in creating illustrations for a leporello format.
The theme that I decides to go after was the sensation of "nothing going your way", and to do that I depicted a girl that constatly gets shot (arrows) in the forhead.
Even so, it ends on a positive note, that you can just take them of and relax for another day.
The style that I was going for was line art with bold but minimal color.
